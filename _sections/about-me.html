Hey there, I'm Lawrence. I am currently a sophomore in the National University of Singapore (NUS), School of Computing, majoring in Computer Science. My undergraduate studies have led me to build my foundation in logic, data structures and algorithms. It has also allowed me to involved in many different project experiences, ranging from building a desktop Java software using best software engineering industry practices, to implementing a poker agent that learns to play optimally against other poker agents in Heads-up (1v1) Limited Hold'em. As per major requires, computer science students in NUS need to specialize in one or two areas. Hence, I am currently taking mainly Artificial Intelligence related modules.

Over the past 2 years, I have worked in start-ups in Singapore and managed several part-time projects. The difficulty I observed from managing a start-up does not daunt me from pursuing my dream of founding my own tech kingdom in the near future. To achieve that, I need to further enhance on my existing software engineering skills and pick up new knowledge during my next internship experience. As I have learnt, start-ups usually have unclear directions and job scopes, I would like to take my next opportunity to gain insights to how a start-up can grow quickly with some objective goals and appealing working environment.

Artifical Intelligence Enthusiast

For the first project that requires us to build a poker agent in the game of Heads-up Limited Hold'em, I have tried coding Minimax Search, Deep Q-Learning and Counterfactual Regret Minimization as approaches even though one will be sufficient.

This is important as growing hydroponic vegetation is a main focus for the future of Singapore.
